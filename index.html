Project Overview

Our Focus was the design and development of an application that transcended boundaries in fashion and heritage globally while bringing the gorgeous art of Africa and the Togolese culture everywhere. Tribe 228 as a diverse brand delivers so much more than just clothing as they welcome the user to their tribe – a way of life, community and the style of breathtaking Africa. Through our intelligently designed mobile app, we enabled Tribe 228 to celebrated African culture globally.

Business Goal

The objective of the Tribe 228 website and mobile application that we built was to increase the sales that, will always remain continuous and constant for any ecommerce business. In order to thrive in the ecommerce industry, you need to boost your sales, constantly. All other objectives are zeroed down to make this objective happen.

Other primary purpose of the platform that we built was to market a platform that not only sell clothing but also make users familiar with the African and Togolese heritage and culture.

Our aim is to enable our clients in becoming a truly global retail brand by retaining their No.1 position in the organized retail sector of the regions they operate in and help them, strive to be the most preferred employer for our current and future multi-ethnic employees.
